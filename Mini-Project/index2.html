BLOG

India made it to the list of top 50 most innovative countries for the first time in 2020 when ranked 48th among 131 countries in the Global Innovation Index (GII). Concerted efforts have been made by both the public and private sector to boost innovation in the country, including through various challenges, initiatives, awards, and hackathons. One such initiative is Engenius – 2022 Best Project Awards, which is a national level annual tech innovation project competition organized by the Directorate of Admissions & Academic Outreach of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University. Engenius offers a platform for talented engineering students and professionals in the engineering industry to showcase their innovative technical projects. The initiative is aimed at building an innovative research and development ecosystem and offering a platform for talented engineering students at UG and PG/PhD level as well as professionals working in the industry to highlight their innovative technical projects.
